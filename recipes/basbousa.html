Basbousa

Home

Basbousa is an amazing desert from the Middle East made with semolina and soaked in a fragrant sugar syrup. This recipe is easy to make and absolutely delicious!

Ingredient List

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp rose water
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup vanilla sugar (or 4 tbsp sugar + 2 tsp vanilla)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup ghee, oil, or butter
  • 1/4 cup milk powder
  • 1/2 cup semolina
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/3 cup desiccated coconut
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt

Step by Step Tutorial

  1. Let's make the syrup
    1. In a small pot combine 1 cup sugar, 1 cup water, 1 tsp lemon juice, and 1 tbsp rose water
    2. Bring to a boil
    3. Leave it cool
  2. Now let's make the batter
    1. Preheat the oven to 170°C/340°F
    2. In a large bowl add in 1/2 cup semolina, 1/2 cup flour, 1/4 cup milk powder, 1/3 cup desiccated coconut, 1 tsp baking powder, 1/2 tsp baking soda, and 1 tsp salt
    3. In a separate bowl whisk together 2 eggs, 1/4 cup vanilla sugar, 1/2 cup yogurt, and 1/2 cup ghee, oil, or melted butter
    4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until well combined
    5. Grease a pan (20x20cm or 8x8 inch or similar)
    6. Pour the batter into the pan
    7. Spread it out evenly
    8. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean
    9. Once out of the oven, immediately pour the cooled syrup over the hot basbousa
    10. Let it soak for at least an hour before serving (the more time soaked the better) and enjoy!
