Lentil Soup

Home

This is my family's favorite soup. This lentil soup recipe is creamy, nourishing and only has 8 ingredients! Ready in less than 30 minutes.

Ingredient List

  • Olive Oil
  • Lentils
  • Water
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Potato
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Salt

Step by Step Tutorial

  1. Rinse 1 cup of lentils in warm water for 5 minutes
  2. Add 2.5 cup of water
  3. Add in the vegetables: 1 medium carrot, 1 onion, 1 small potato, and 1 tomato, 2 cloves of garlic
  4. Add a tablespoon of salt
  5. Drizzle some olive oil
  6. Bring the heat to low-medium for 30 minutes or until the lentils are soft and cooked through
  7. Puree with a blender, food processor or hand blender and serve

    8. Optional - serve topped with bread and lemons
