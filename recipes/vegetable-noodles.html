Vegetable Noodles

Home

This is a quick and easy vegetable noodles recipe that's perfect for a healthy meal. It's packed with vegetables and has a delicious sauce.

Ingredient List

  • 1 package of noodles (any type)
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, broccoli)
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of ginger, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil

Step by Step Tutorial

  1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a wok or large pan, heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, saut√© for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 5 minutes until tender-crisp.
  5. Add the cooked noodles to the pan.
  6. Add soy sauce and sesame oil, toss everything together until well coated.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
